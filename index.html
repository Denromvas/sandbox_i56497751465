Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 30, 2024

This Privacy Policy describes how we handle information in our Halloween Live Wallpaper application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

Important: Our app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

1. Information Collection

The app requires the following permissions:

  • Storage Access Permission (READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE / READ_MEDIA_IMAGES)
    • Used only when you choose to select a custom background image
    • We do not access any other files on your device
    • Selected images are stored locally on your device only
  • Set Wallpaper Permission (SET_WALLPAPER)
    • Required to set the live wallpaper on your device
    • Used only when you apply the wallpaper

2. Data Storage and Usage

Our app:

  • Stores only your selected wallpaper preferences locally on your device
  • Does not collect any personal information
  • Does not access the internet
  • Does not share any data with third parties
  • Does not use analytics services
  • Does not create user profiles
  • Does not track user behavior

3. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Use the app without providing any personal information
  • Remove the app and all its locally stored preferences at any time
  • Choose whether to grant or deny storage permissions

4. Children's Privacy

Our app is suitable for all ages and does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the new policy.
